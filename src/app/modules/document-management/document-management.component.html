Every good quality manager knows that the management and control of documents is extremely important in any quality management system. Food safety managers know it is just as important in a food safety management system.

HACCP Now provides the tools for quality and food safety managers to author, approve, securely store and distribute important documents such as quality and food safety procedures.

The system manages version control and allows approval processes to be defined so that senior management sign-off can take place without the need to request a meeting and align diaries.

This approach removes delays and provides a full audit trail of document versions required by quality and food safety standards. It also allows collaboration on authoring and approving documentation and makes the audit process simple, fast and effective.

Documents created in HACCP Now can be linked directly to any level within the HACCP plan which, in turn, allows electronic publishing of a fully integrated and cross referenced food safety plan.
