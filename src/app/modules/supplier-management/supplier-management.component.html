The important areas of supplier management, from a food safety and quality perspective, revolve around assurance and approval to supply one or more products or raw materials. They also impact traceability, supplier audits and supplier non conformity management.

HACCP Now provides a Supplier Management module that allows your business to ensure that it stays on top of supplier approvals through audits and automated questionnaires. It also ensures that product approvals are tracked and renewals are automated, where possible and relevant. The system tracks non conformities logged against a supplier so that their performance can easily be monitored.

The Supplier Raw Material portal (see Raw Material Control) integrates with the Supplier Management module in order to have the supplier keep their information up to date in order to reduce the time and overhead of your business having to do most of the work in maintaining records relevant to suppliers. It also gives you tools to track assurance status and emerging issues with particular suppliers.
